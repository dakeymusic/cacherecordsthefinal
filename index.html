About

We are a bunch of happy boys who like happy music. We also like drums and bass, sometimes harmonica. All this combined, we decided on a dark evening in January 2019 to open a record label. Alex said, "My favorite artist is Drake!" The rest of the group joined, We called Drake the same evening. Guess if Drake was in the notes? Since that evening in January, everything is history.
